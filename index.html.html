The new ADV 150 has many similarities with the current PCX 150 and we can speculate
that both of these scooters share the same platform. It is powered by the same 150cc powerplant,
has a fully-digital instrument panel, LED lights, and comes with a choice of either ABS or Honda's
combi-brake system.

For convenience, it is fitted with Honda's Smart Key System, a large 28-liter under-seat
box which can accommodate a full-face helmet and other assortments, shaped similarly to the
PCX 150. Fuel tank capacity is 8-liters, giving it a range of approximately 350 kilometers in one top-up,
just like the PCX 150.
